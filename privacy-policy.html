1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Duc Nguyen ("Operator", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit nguyenminhduc9988.github.io and any associated subdomains (collectively, the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data including page views, device type, browser, and general geographic location.
  • Server logs: Our hosting providers (GitHub Pages, Hetzner) may collect standard server log data (IP address, request path, user agent, timestamp).

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Tool Data Handling

AI tools on the Site process your inputs in real-time to generate responses. Tool inputs are:

  • Not permanently stored on our servers (processed in-memory only).
  • May be transmitted to third-party AI API providers for processing.
  • Not used to train AI models unless the API provider's terms specify otherwise.

For specific tool data handling, refer to the relevant AI provider's privacy policy:
