What I Build

I focus on a few principles that shape every project:

  • Privacy by default. My online tools are 100% client-side. No data is sent to a server, no tracking of what you type, no sign-up required. What you compute stays on your machine.
  • Zero dependencies where possible. My open-source libraries use only the Python standard library or browser-native APIs. This keeps them fast, secure, and easy to audit.
  • Depth over volume. I'd rather write one thorough guide that solves a problem completely than ten shallow ones. The article archive reflects real debugging and research, not generated filler.

Open Source
