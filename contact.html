Before You Reach Out

  • Is it a bug? Include the tool name, your browser, the steps to reproduce, and what you expected vs. what happened. Screenshots help a lot.
  • Is it a feature idea? Tell me the problem you're trying to solve — that's more useful than a specific feature request.
  • Is it about a guide? Mention the article title and the section, so I can find it quickly.

Response Time

I'm based in Singapore (UTC+8) and work on these projects alongside other commitments. I aim to acknowledge bug reports within a few days, though complex fixes may take longer. Thank you for your patience — and thank you for using the tools.

Privacy
